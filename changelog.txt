# Thaumic Concilium 1.1.1
- FIXES:
  - Empty NBT tag no longer applied to dropped blocks and items.
  - Added black list support for Sloppy Alchemist and Burned Witch.
  - Correct handling of missing required mods instead of crash.
  - Explicit floor generation for Burning Site.
  - Crimson Avanpost' chest is now a legit loot table, modify "ThaumicConcilium:cultist_tower" with your loot editor of choice.

# Thaumic Concilium 1.1.0
- !!!BREAKING, READ BEFORE UPDATING!!!
  -  Config values for mob replacement and rift spawn chances are now defined in a sane way (lesser number - lesser chance). Reverse them in your configs accordingly.

- NEW:
  - Thaumic Concilium:
    - Remains of Nether Explorers can now be found in the lava lakes of the Nether.
  - Witchery:
    - Burned Witches can be found flying in the Nether.
    - Burning Sites can be found in villages.
      - Blood Magic:
        - Chorts can be found galloping in the Nether.
  - Thaumic Dyes:
    - Crimson Paladins may spawn with Crimson Paladin Helm.
    - Crimson Cult' avanposts, guarded by Crimson Archers.
    - Crimson Rangers now roam the world, looking for eldritch secrets.
  - Botania & Alfheim:
    - Remains of Withered Botanists can now be found in the Overworld and Alfheim.
  - Ars Magica 2 & Tainted Magic:
    - Crimson Raid spell component can now be learned.
  - Thaumic Horizons & Tainted Magic:
    - Method to improve capabilities of ethereal state.
  - Gadomancy:
    - Alfheim:
      - Tinctura effect for Aura Pylon.
    - Avaritia:
      - Terminus effect for Aura Pylon. 

- FIXES:
  - Fixed mob spawn with biome adding modifications (now configurable).
  - Fixed Rift Gem teleportation to/from End.
  - Pontifex Robe now can be dyed.
  - Added hints on traded Thaumaturge's researches and on researches dropped from Mad Thaumaturges.
  - Learning Curses from Crimson Cult research now automatically gives Witchery knowledge as well. Ask admin to type /concilium witcheryRepair Player if it doesn't.
  - Vengeful Golem now actually dings with a bell.
  - Vengeful Golem now can't affect player wearing Earmuffs from Witchery.
  - Vengeful Golem now only attacks those who he can see.
  - Void Reality blocks can no longer be exploded or replaced.
  - Vis Conductor focus can now be enchanted with Potency, that speeds up consuming vis from Destabilized Crystal Clusters. 
  - Quicksilver Elemental now applies non-contagious version of Vis Exhaust.
  - Quicksilver Elemental now becomes vulnerable when under slowness effect. Removed "Quicksilver immortality" config option.
  - Tight Belt now updates only once in 3 seconds.
  - Crimson Pontifex' AoE lifesteal attack now causes mob damage instead of magic damage.
  - Added warp to some researches.
  - Dissolved now moves slower.
  - Decreased movement speed and attack power of Paranoid Warriors. Changed damage divisors for shadow/void/thaumium variants from 25/20/15 to 10/5/3.5, improved their AI a bit.
  - Default mob spawn chances are lowered a bit.
  - Lowered Alive Gibs' damage.
  - Changed default special key for Pontifex Robe from H to L.
  - Bottle Of Clear Water now extinguishes blocks of fire as well.
  - Burdening Amulet infusion recipe no longer requires Sensus in infusion process.
  - Maybe something else I forgor to record.
